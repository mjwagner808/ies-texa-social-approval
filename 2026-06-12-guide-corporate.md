# How to Review Social Media Posts — Your Step-by-Step Guide

**IES-TEXA — Corporate Review Portal**
Prepared by Anthology FINN Partners
Last updated: June 15, 2026

---

## What Is This?

Before certain IES-TEXA social media posts go live, they come to you for final approval. Local client teams have already reviewed and approved them — your review is the last step.

This guide walks you through how to access your portal, review posts, and send your decisions back in one summary.

You don't need an account or password. We'll send you a link, and that's all you need.

---

## Step 1: Open Your Email

When posts are ready for your final review, you'll receive an email from **Anthology FINN Partners** with the subject line:

> **Your approval is needed**

The email will list the posts waiting for your review and include a blue button that says **Review all posts**.

Click that button. It will open your review portal in a web browser.

---

## Step 2: See What's Waiting for You

Your portal shows a calendar with posts for the month.

Posts that **need your review** have a purple badge that says **Your review needed**.

Click on any post card to open it and see the full details.

---

## Step 3: Review a Post

When you click a post, a panel opens on the right side of the screen. Here you'll see:

- The **post title** and scheduled date
- Which **platforms** it's going to (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn)
- The **post copy** — the text that will be published
- Any **images or media** attached to the post
- A **Discussion** section — this shows any previous feedback from the local client and agency

Take your time to review everything, including the discussion thread. It provides context about any changes that were already made before reaching you.

---

## Step 4: Add a Note (Optional)

If you want to leave a note with your decision, type it in the box above the buttons. This is optional for approvals, but required if you're requesting changes — please describe what needs to be different.

---

## Step 5: Take Action

At the bottom of the panel, you'll see two buttons:

### If the post is ready to go:
Click **Approve**

You'll see a confirmation message. Your decision is saved.

### If something needs to change:
1. Type your feedback in the comment box above the buttons
2. Click **Request Changes**

Your feedback is saved and will be included when you send your responses.

---

## Step 6: Send Your Responses

After reviewing and acting on your posts, a button will appear at the top of the portal:

> **⚠️ X ready — send responses!**

Click **Send Responses** to deliver all of your decisions in one summary to the local client and the Anthology FINN Partners agency team.

**This step is required.** No one is notified of your decisions until you click this button. You can review multiple posts across multiple sessions before sending — everything queues up and goes out together when you're ready.

After you click it, you'll see a green confirmation — "✓ All caught up" — and the badge will disappear.

---

## Step 7: After You Submit

After sending your responses:

- Posts you approved move to **Approved** (green)
- Posts you asked to change return to the agency for revisions
- Local client and the agency both receive one summary email with all your decisions

---

## What the Different Badges Mean

| Badge Color | What It Means |
|-------------|---------------|
| Purple — "Your review needed" | This post is waiting for your feedback |
| Teal — "Changes submitted" | You requested changes; the team is working on it |
| Green — "Approved" | All reviews are done |
| Dark green — "Published" | The post has been posted live |

---

## After You Request Changes

When you request changes, the post moves to a "Changes submitted" state (teal badge). It will remain visible on your calendar so you know it's being worked on.

Once the agency has made the revisions, the post will return to your queue with a purple "Your review needed" badge.

---

## Changed Your Mind?

If you submitted a decision and need to reverse it before sending your responses:

1. Click the post to open it
2. At the bottom, look for a small link that says **Undo my approval** or **Undo my request**
3. Click it — your decision is reversed and you can review the post again

> This option is available as long as you haven't yet sent your responses for that post. If the button isn't there, contact Anthology FINN Partners directly.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need to log in or create an account?**
No. Your link is your access. Bookmark it or save the email for future reference.

**What if I lose the link?**
Contact the Anthology FINN Partners team and we'll resend it.

**Will I see posts that haven't been reviewed by the local team yet?**
No. You'll only see posts that have already been reviewed and approved at the local level, plus posts you've already acted on.

**How do I know when new posts are ready?**
You'll receive an email from us. You don't need to check the portal manually.

**Can I see feedback that the local client gave?**
Yes. The Discussion section in each post panel shows all comments from the review process, so you have full context.

**Do I have to send all my responses at once?**
No. You can review posts across multiple sessions and send your responses whenever you're ready. Everything queues up until you click "Send Responses."

**I need to flag something about a post that's already been approved. What do I do?**
Contact the Anthology FINN Partners team directly — we can reopen the review if needed.
