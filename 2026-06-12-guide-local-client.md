# How to Review Social Media Posts — Your Step-by-Step Guide

**IES-TEXA — Local Client Review Portal**
Prepared by Anthology FINN Partners
Last updated: June 15, 2026

---

## What Is This?

Before any IES-TEXA social media post goes live, we send it to you for review. This guide walks you through how to approve posts, request changes, or ask a question — all in one place.

You don't need to create an account or remember a password. We'll send you a link, and that's all you need.

---

## Step 1: Open Your Email

When posts are ready for your review, you'll receive an email from **Anthology FINN Partners** with the subject line:

> **Your approval is needed**

The email will list the posts waiting for your review and include a blue button that says **Review all posts**.

Click that button. It will open your review portal in a web browser.

---

## Step 2: See What's Waiting for You

Your portal shows a calendar with all your posts for the month.

Posts that **need your review** have an orange badge that says **Your review needed**.

Click on any post card to open it and see the full details.

---

## Step 3: Review a Post

When you click a post, a panel opens on the right side of the screen. Here you'll see:

- The **post title** and scheduled date
- Which **platforms** it's going to (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn)
- The **post copy** — the text that will be published
- Any **images or media** attached to the post
- A **Discussion** section showing any previous notes or comments

Take your time to review everything.

---

## Step 4: Take Action

At the bottom of the panel, you'll see two buttons:

### If everything looks good:
Click **Approve**

You'll see a confirmation screen that says "Mahalo!" and the post is marked ready for corporate review.

### If you need changes:
1. In the box above the buttons, type what you'd like changed — be as specific as you can
2. Then click **Request Changes**

Your feedback is automatically sent to the Anthology FINN Partners team. They'll make the revisions and send the post back to you when it's ready.

> **You don't need to click anything else.** Just type your note and click "Request Changes" — that's one step, not two.

---

## Step 5: Send to Corporate

Once you've approved all the posts in your queue, a button will appear at the top of the portal:

> **⚠️ X ready — send to Corporate!**

Click **Send to Corporate** to notify the corporate team that posts are ready for their final review.

**This step is required.** Corporate won't receive any email until you click this button. The number on the badge tells you how many posts are waiting to be sent.

After you click it, you'll see a green confirmation — "✓ All caught up" — and the badge will disappear.

---

## Step 6: After You Submit

After approving or requesting changes on each post, you'll see a green confirmation screen.

- Click **Back to portal** to review other posts
- The calendar updates automatically

---

## What the Different Badges Mean

| Badge Color | What It Means |
|-------------|---------------|
| Orange — "Your review needed" | This post is waiting for your feedback |
| Teal — "Agency is revising" | Your changes were noted; the team is working on it |
| Indigo — "Send to Corporate" | You approved it; tap the toolbar button to notify corporate |
| Purple — "In corporate review" | Post is with the corporate team |
| Green — "Approved" | All reviews are done |
| Dark green — "Published" | The post has been posted live |

---

## What Happens After You Send to Corporate

Corporate reviews posts on their own schedule. When they finish, they'll send back a summary of their decisions — all at once. At that point:

- Posts they approved move to "Approved" (green)
- Posts they asked to change return to the agency for revisions

You'll receive one summary email from us with all of their decisions.

---

## Changed Your Mind?

If you submitted an approval or change request and want to reverse it:

1. Click the post to open it
2. At the bottom, you'll see a small link that says **Undo my approval** or **Undo my request**
3. Click it — your decision is reversed and you can review the post again

> This only works as long as the post hasn't moved too far forward in the process. If the button isn't there, contact Anthology FINN Partners directly.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need to log in?**
No. Your link is your access. Bookmark it or save the email so you can find it again.

**What if I lose the link?**
Contact the Anthology FINN Partners team and we'll resend it.

**Can I see posts that have already been approved?**
Yes — the calendar shows all posts at every stage. Green and dark green posts are already handled.

**How do I know when new posts are ready?**
We'll send you an email. You don't need to check the portal manually.

**I accidentally approved something. Can I fix it?**
Yes — see "Changed Your Mind?" above. If the undo option isn't available, contact us right away.

**Can I leave a general comment without approving or requesting changes?**
At this time, comments are submitted along with a decision (approve or request changes). If you have a question before deciding, email your Anthology FINN Partners contact directly.
